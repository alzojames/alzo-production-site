About Me

I hold a significant amount of excitement and fondness for the fields of computer science, programming, and technology, because of the simple fact that working with software and script affords me the capability to create a plethora of different things, whether it be code for code that enables the operation of a computerized system that monitors the salinity of water samples or modifications for various components of a computer build that significantly increase the operational efficiency. Furthermore than the extraordinary ability to create that my interest in technology allows me is the tantalizing challenge that each project poses to me as a scientist and a scholar. Every task that I will complete using my technological skills will pose me with a situation that will require me to employ my problem solving skills and will train me to be more prepared and effective with concerns to the field of technology.

Photography

Outside of computer science my passion lays in photography. I first realized my passion for photography during my senior year in high school when a friend let me use his camera for a while. For the past 3 years I have used photography as my way of forgetting the stresses that life brings. I have grown a lot in the art over the years but I still have a long way to go. I enjoy shooting cars, street photography and nature photography but I’m generally open to other kinds of photography.

Cars

It might be because of Need for Speed, Fast and Furious or Top Gear but I love cars. I enjoy the occasional car meet and working on cars with my friends. In the past year I have joined my passion for passion for photography with cars and started shooting cars and making car videos with people I’ve met at car meets.
